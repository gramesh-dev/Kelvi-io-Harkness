This week's story
Arya made friends with the triangle's secret.

She spent three sessions on Geometry Planet, exploring the idea that the three corners of a triangle always add up to something surprising. Her first instinct was "they probably add up to something different for each triangle" — a reasonable guess.

On Wednesday she tore the corners off a paper triangle and put them side by side. "Wait. That looks like a straight line." She tested it on three different triangles. Same thing every time. That pattern owned her attention for twenty minutes.

She also tried Puzzle Planet for the first time and solved the river-crossing puzzle three different ways.
