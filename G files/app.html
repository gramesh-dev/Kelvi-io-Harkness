How Kelvi classifies student thinking

P/C/G describes the kind of thinking a student is doing in a moment — not a rank or grade. Students move between levels within a single problem.

${[['P — Procedural','Following steps, applying a method'],['C — Conceptual','Explaining why, seeing the underlying structure'],['G — Generative','Extending, generalizing, asking new questions']].map(([label,desc])=>`
${label}
${desc}
`).join('')}
