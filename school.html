HOW IT WORKS

From your classroom to their kitchen table.

You create the assignment. Your students discuss. The conversation can continue at home. You see all of it.

1
You create
Design an assignment or assessment your way. Kelvi helps if you want — or stay with what you know works.
2
Students discuss
Students work the problem in class. The assignment generates mathematical conversation — at tables, in pairs, on paper.
3
Home continues
Teachers share a homework link. Students can open Kelvi at home and continue working with the Kelvi thinking partner that never gives full solutions.
4
You see it all
The dashboard visualizes student thinking — from class work and home sessions. No student falls through the cracks.

Kelvi is not a curriculum. It's not a textbook replacement. It's the layer that sits on top of whatever you already teach — and makes the thinking visible.

OUR DEFAULT LENS

P/C/G — how Kelvi reads student thinking.

Kelvi's default framework classifies student thinking from following steps to creating new mathematics. It's how the dashboard sees — not how you have to teach.

P
PROCEDURAL
Following steps. Applying formulas. "How do I do this?"
C
CONCEPTUAL
Explaining meaning. Connecting ideas. "Why does this work?"
G
GENERATIVE
Creating and extending. "What if I changed this?"

Your district uses a different framework? Kelvi adapts.

THE DASHBOARD

The most powerful tool is seeing your students think.

Upload classwork. Connect home sessions. The dashboard reads every student's work so you can focus on the ones who need you most.

Thinking distribution at a glance. How many students are at each level on every assignment. See the shape of your class.
Student view
Individual trajectory over time. Strongest moments quoted from their actual work. Where they grew, where they're stuck.
Intervention alerts
Students who have been stuck for multiple assignments. You know exactly who needs you — before they fall behind.
