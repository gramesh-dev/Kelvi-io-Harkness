About Kelvi School
An AI teaching partner that shows you how your students think — not just what they get right.

The P/C/G framework

Every student interaction is classified as Procedural (knows the steps), Conceptual (understands why), or Generative (creates new questions from answers). This maps directly to Hattie's Surface/Deep/Transfer learning model.

The Question Circle

A five-step classroom discussion protocol: Problem Drop → Small Group → Kelvi enters as a group resource → Whole Class → Individual Close. Runs in 30–35 minutes inside Kelvi School.

Kelvi Homework

Students continue at home on Kelvi Homework. Their sessions feed back into your dashboard — you see classroom and home thinking in one place.

What Kelvi never does

Kelvi never gives answers. It asks the question the student needed to ask themselves. Mistakes are treated as thinking in progress, not failures.
